When When Dual Combinations Are Not Enough

Reductions in SeDBP & SeSBP with Tripl Combination were Rapid (Within 2 Weeks Of Switch) and Sustained To Week 12 Compared with Dual Component(All P < 0.001)

The number of antihypertensive agents needed to achieve target BP levels in seven large-scale controlled trials.
